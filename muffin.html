About My Dog!

Hi! My name is Jennifer and this is my dog Muffin! She is a tiny brown, black, and white dog. She's very special because she is a Manchester Terrior and Yorkie mix. My page will tell you about my dog, Muffin, and the similarities and differences between her and her two individual breeds.

Muffin is 2-3 years old and loves to sleep. If she could she would sleep whole day. She is a big fan of cuddling, and loves going for walks sporting her famous blue and black leash. Her favorite toys are any type of stuffed animals, especially the teddy bear my boyfriend got me. Even though she's already bitten through basically the entire bear she still goes at it with it. A daily part of routine is enjoying her dry dog food.
